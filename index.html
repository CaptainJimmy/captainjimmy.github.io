About Me


I am a former Software Engineer who took a 14 year hiatus to pursue a career as a USCG License Master Captain. I have been working professionally on my Captain’s license, but I am eager to return to technology as a career. I have never been far from tech, and I maintained a lot of my skills that made me successful in my careers both in technology and as a Captain. Prior to getting my Captain’s license, I specialized in UNIX Shell Scripting, Administration, and complex software integration. I am currently enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp for Full Stack Flex Web Development, working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Linux, Apache, and all the tools associated. The school and it's materials are unrelenting work, but it is preparing me very well for a return to what I love: technology. I can provide my Professional Captain’s resume upon request.
